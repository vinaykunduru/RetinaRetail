Our Story

Our Journey to Excellence

Founded on the principles of quality and innovation, Retina Retail was created to bring vibrant transformations to homes and businesses across India. With years of expertise in the paint industry and a commitment to empowering local entrepreneurs, we’re here to help you create a profitable future.

Mission

To provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the tools, support, and proven business models needed to succeed in the decorative paint industry.

Vision

To become India’s most trusted name in paint retailing, inspiring growth and innovation in every corner of the country.
